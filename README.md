### About Us



Welcome to **BCAS-Team.github.io** – a student-led, educational initiative focused on ethical hacking, digital freedom, scripting, and technology history. Our goal is to responsibly explore the undercurrents of the internet and share tools, guides, and historical knowledge in a moderated and legal-safe environment.



---



### What'S New In Beta 1.5.1?



Beta 1.5.1 is a **safety and compliance update**, refining the platform’s scope, security, and legality:



* 🧹 **Drug Wiki Removed:** In an effort to reduce liability, the Drug Wiki—while educational—has been removed due to the inclusion of dosage content.

* ❌ **PiVPN Section Removed:** All content related to PiVPN has been permanently removed.

* 📜 **Legal Page Created:** A full `legal.html` page has been implemented to outline the limits, disclaimers, and terms of our platform.

* 🔞 **18+ Enforcement Clarified:** All sensitive content remains behind age verification.

* 🧠 **Roadmap Alignment:** Legal policy now accounts for future content, including scripting, device liberation, crypto privacy, and digital activism.
